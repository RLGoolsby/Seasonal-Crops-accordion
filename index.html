Planting Seasons 

It's possible to garden all year round

  • Winter

    • Winter

      Winter is a great time to plant crops that can withstand the cold.

      These crops are hardy and can survive in colder temperatures, making them ideal for winter planting.

      • Leafy Greens

        • Spinach
        • Kale
        • Lettuce
        • Collards

      • Allium

        • Onion
        • Garlic
        • Leeks
        • Scallions

      • Root Crops

        • Carrots
        • Beets
        • Sweet Potatoes
        • Rutabagas
